Research

Theory of Mind & Motion Perception

My research interests lie at the intersection of theory of mind and motion perception. Using fMRI and computational modeling, I particularly study how humans read intentions from others' actions and attribute mental states to them.

Software for Neuroscience

To promote reproducibility in neuroscience research, I develop pipelines based on the Brain Imaging Data Structure. Here is my latest work PyMVPA BIDS-App, a pipeline that performs MultiVariate Pattern Analysis (MVPA) on fMRI data.
