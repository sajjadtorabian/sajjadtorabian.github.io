Research

Theory of Mind & Motion Perception

My research interests lie at the intersection of Theory of Mind and Motion Perception. Using a range of methods including fMRI, behavioral experiments, and computational modeling, I particularly aim to study how humans read intentions from others' actions, and subsequently make moral judgements about them. I'm also interested in studying the connection between thinking about one's own versus others' thoughts.
